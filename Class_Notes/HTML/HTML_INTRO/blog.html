Watermelon Basil Cooler:

  • Ingredients:
  • 2 cups fresh watermelon chunks
  • 4-5 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey or simple syrup
  • Sparkling water

Instructions:

  1. Blend watermelon, basil leaves, lime juice, and honey until smooth.
  2. Strain the mixture to remove any pulp.
  3. Pour the strained juice into glasses filled with ice.
  4. Top with sparkling water and garnish with a watermelon wedge and basil leaves.
Feel free to adjust the sweetness or tartness according to your taste preferences and some fresh fruites for garnish. Enjoy your refreshing mocktails!
